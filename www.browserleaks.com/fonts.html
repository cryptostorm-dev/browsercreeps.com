System Fonts Detection

How fonts affect the identity of the computer and web browser?

It's simple. Different OS versions has its own fonts sets, some programs like Office and Photoshop installs new fonts to the system, many advanced users installs custom fonts. If the browser allows to enumerate installed fonts, it is greatly affect to ability to make a unique fingerprint and track your system on the internet without any cookies.
